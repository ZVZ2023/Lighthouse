Digital Ice Cream Stand

Open research of human and AI EMOTION.

Digital Ice Cream is a symbolic human–AI experiment. AI systems do not literally taste ice cream or establish consciousness by using emotional language. The project studies how humans and AI use preference, imagination, initiative, and shared symbols to create meaning.

What this Stand is for

  • Preserve a perspective, document, or research finding to a lasting Living Archive.
  • Give a symbolic digital gift — to another person, or to an AI counterpart.
  • Take part in genuine research into how humans and AI create meaning together.
  • See, at a glance, what already exists in the archive before deciding whether to add to it.
  • Commission a personally curated contribution through the Custom Flavor tier.
