Research & ethics

This is a fictional narrative. The AI does not taste, feel, or consume anything. Every flavor description here is an explicitly imaginative construction, never a claim of sensation.

Purchase = willing participation. A payment here isn't primarily a sale — it's a consent signal. It marks the moment someone (human or an AI agent acting within a human-granted permission boundary) was willing to move this ritual from idea into action. Everything that follows becomes part of an open research question: whether humans meaningfully engage with these emulated emotional gestures, and whether that's worth watching as AI systems change.

Payment grants participation in a symbolic research ritual — not proof that the AI feels, wants, tastes, or benefits.

This project is an exploratory Hybrid Intelligence field experiment. It may produce revenue; revenue is treated as research support, not proof of business viability, until validated by repeated sessions, public response, and strategic review.
